About Jeff Thoensen

QA engineer, context-driven testing, Playwright, CI, and exploratory work

I work in QA with a focus on mitigating risk. I write Playwright tests where they help, wire them into CI, and spend most of my time exploring the product to catch problems early. I keep suites small and maintain the code with reviews and refactors.

Most of my work is in JavaScript and TypeScript, with some Python. Tools I use often are Playwright, GitHub Actions, and Allure.
